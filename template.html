Shipping

We offer FREE domestic Shipping on all items within Republic Of Ireland!

Your item will be carefully packed and dispatched within 24 hours of receiving payment (Mon - Fri). Please allow 2-3 working days from day of purchase for your item to be delivered.

All orders received before 3:00pm will be dispatched on the same day if payment is confirmed.

Return

If you are not happy with your purchase, please contact us within 14 days of delivery to authorise the return. Once confirmed, please return the item, in its original packaging, within 30 days of delivery. Upon receipt, we will inspect the item and process the refund via PayPal. Any and all costs, for postage returns incurred by the buyer, will not be refunded. Any damage caused to the manufacturer's packaging will result in rejection of the return, this includes the manufacturer's seal being broken. We reserve the right to add a 25% re-stocking fee, at our discretion, for items returned that were wrongly ordered or no longer needed.

If your item is faulty or you have received the incorrect product please contact us immediately using the eBay messaging system. Our Communication systems are monitored 9am - 5pm (Mon. - Fri.)
